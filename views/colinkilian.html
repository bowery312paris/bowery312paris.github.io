Colin Kilian

Lives and works in Kauai, Hawaï

Colin Kilian’s paintings are a vehicle for exploring and sharing phenomena that he experiences subconsciously and during meditation. Since this subject matter is generally fluid and extra dimensional in nature, the paintings have a strong quality of being in motion. The general aesthetic is partially driven by his education and work experience in design. Kilian's intention is to create works that are easily accessible because of their visual dynamism – and which unfold into deeper and more complex levels of meaning the longer one looks at them.
